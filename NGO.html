About Us


“A companion in need is a nestam in fact”.
Yes, one who helps us in the need is a real friend. And it is also factual that our society comprises a lot of serving hands just about us, fortunately. Still, we need some more support physically, emotionally most of the times, and monetarily. The repair we get on time is more imperative for us to reckon. How many of us are accessible to dole out others when they are in the real need? God gives opportunity to very few on these occasions. We feel God has given us this chance, now, at this instant to help very few, may not be all.
Hence the title chosen…………..
"Nestam- mana kosam manam"

Read more...

Philanthropy is an idea that has been in existence for eons. In society, no matter how hard people try, not everyone can be equal. In India, this is a truth that is seen every day. Countless numbers of young, talented people strive to rise above this condition—but that is not always possible. Quality education is expensive and often times, the brightest minds come from the most humble backgrounds. Of course, there is no shortage of organizations that try to help, and for many years, many of our Board members contributed to their cause.

They all could have continued to do so. All of them were doing well in life and they could have simply contented themselves with the knowledge that “someone somewhere” was benefiting from their contribution. That all changed however, after seeing the very same education problem in their hometown. While the education rate had somewhat increased in the grade school level, higher education was still not completed by over half of the new generation. A “tenth class” degree (high school diploma) is still thought to be more than enough education by most families in India today. Seeing the prevalence of this issue, we decided to do something about it. Malcom X once said, “Education is our passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to the people who prepare for it today.” Education is something that is supposed to transcend money, caste, and social standing. It is a phenomenon that unites the people and makes the world a better place. Having a good education can give anyone the power to change their path for the better, which in turn, will make them change the mindset of others as well. In December 2011, the same ideas were discussed at the Class of 1986 Municipal High School Reunion and after some deliberation, “Nestam – Mana kosam manam”, Charitable organization was born. Our personal mission is to give bright students in poverty the chance to achieve their dream of going to college. We want to open doors for them, doors to better places where they can improve their families’ conditions. We hope that through our project, higher education will be appreciated both by the students and their parents. We are NESTAM and we want your child to succeed.
